This plot shows all the film's posters from the Criterion Collection dataset. The plot has an X axis by lightness and Y axis by Hue. The thumb width is 153px and the thumb height is 190px
We can observe that there's a majority of posters that are black or dark, then a lot of blue and red posters and finally white.
Also, there green posters are minority and they're represented by faces.
This datavisualisation allows to realize that some posters are extremly white or black.
Furthermore, the Criterion collection is formed by a great diversity of movies with red and yellow posters
